                                                                                                                                                                                              Total Compensation

                                                                                                                                                                                              We value our employees and appreciate the time and effort they commit to ensuring students in Palm Beach have access to an excellent education. At Palm Beach Public Schools, compensation is so much more than salary. Our total compensation tool can help you calculate your earning potential.

                                                                                                                                                                                              Disclaimer: Total compensation calculations are for demonstration and estimation purposes only. Actual salaries will be determined upon hire and assignment.

                                                                                                                                                                                              The list of special assignments included in this calculator is not exhaustive. All special assignments are subject to principal assignment and availability

                                                                                                                                                                                              School psychologists, occupational/physical therapists, audiologists, and speech pathologists are not included in this calculator. Starting salary for these roles is $49,680 and varies based on certifications and applicable experience.
